# Welcome to My Creative Space! 👋

Hi, I'm **Adithyan M**, and this is my interactive digital portfolio. Instead of just a plain resume, I wanted to build something that feels like a cinematic experience—a place where you can explore my work, my journey, and my skills in a fun and engaging way.

> [!NOTE]
> **Experience it live here:** [adithyan-portfolio.pages.dev](https://adithyan-portfolio.pages.dev/)

---

## 🌟 What You’ll Find Inside

### 🤖 Meet Zippy, Your Guide
When you first enter the site, you'll be greeted by **Zippy**, my friendly robotic assistant. He’ll introduce you to the space and help you find your way around. He even reacts to how you move and scroll!

### 🕹️ An Interactive Terminal
For those who like a bit of a "tech" feel, I’ve built a mini-computer system right into the site. You can type simple commands to jump to different sections, check my status, or even find hidden secrets I’ve tucked away.

### 🌊 Smooth & Cinematic Exploration
I’ve designed the entire website to feel "buttery smooth." As you scroll, you’ll see high-quality animations and a cinematic background that changes with you, making it feel more like you're watching a movie than reading a webpage.

### 🎮 The "Bug Smasher" Game
If you look closely enough, you might find a little game hidden in the site. It’s a fun way to interact with the page while you're learning about what I do.

### ✉️ Let's Connect!
Whether you’re a recruiter, a fellow developer, or just someone with a cool idea, I’d love to hear from you. The site has a built-in contact system that sends your messages directly to me in real-time.
